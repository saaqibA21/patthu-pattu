# பத்து பாட்டு நூல்கள் - Pathu Pattu Website

## 🌟 Overview

This is a **premium, feature-rich website** dedicated to the Pathu Pattu (Ten Classical Tamil Literary Works) from the Sangam Era. The website showcases ancient Tamil literature with modern web design aesthetics.

## ✨ Key Features

### 1. **Clickable Book Cards with Detailed Modals**
- **10 Interactive Book Cards**: Each of the ten books (திருமுருகாற்றுப்படை, பொருநராற்றுப்படை, etc.) is displayed as a beautiful card
- **Click to Explore**: Click on any book card to open a detailed modal with:
  - Full description in Tamil and English
  - Poet information
  - Number of lines
  - Historical period
  - Main themes
  - Additional context and details
- **Smooth Animations**: Cards have hover effects, scale animations, and gradient overlays

### 2. **Bilingual Support (Tamil/English)**
- **Language Toggle**: Switch between Tamil (தமிழ்) and English instantly
- **Dynamic Content**: All text updates automatically when you change language
- **Seamless Experience**: Books, themes, timeline, and all sections support both languages

### 3. **Audio Player with Playlist**
- **Beautiful Audio Player**: Modern player design with play/pause/next/previous controls
- **Playlist**: 5 traditional recitations of Pathu Pattu works
- **Progress Bar**: Visual progress indicator
- **Time Display**: Shows current time and duration
- **Active Track Highlighting**: Currently playing song is highlighted in the playlist

### 4. **Historical Timeline**
- **Visual Timeline**: Beautiful timeline showing the Sangam era history
- **Key Events**: 
  - 300 BCE: Sangam Era Begins
  - 200 BCE: Pathu Pattu Composition
  - 200 CE: End of Sangam Era
  - 1800s: Rediscovery by U.V. Swaminatha Iyer
- **Animated Cards**: Timeline items animate on hover

### 5. **Gallery Section**
- **6 Gallery Items**: Showcasing:
  - Palm leaf manuscripts
  - Murugan temples
  - Ancient coins
  - Tamil landscapes
  - U.V. Swaminatha Iyer
  - Archaeological artifacts
- **Hover Effects**: Images zoom and overlay information appears
- **Gradient Placeholders**: Beautiful color-coded placeholders with emojis

### 6. **Search Functionality**
- **Smart Search**: Search across all books
- **Real-time Filtering**: Results update as you type
- **Animated Search Bar**: Expands when clicked

### 7. **AI Chat Assistant**
- **Interactive Chat**: Click the chat button (💬) in the bottom right
- **Knowledge Base**: Ask questions about Pathu Pattu
- **Bilingual Responses**: Responds in Tamil or English based on your language setting
- **Smooth Animations**: Chat window slides up with beautiful animations

### All 10 Books with Complete Details:

2. பொருநராற்றுப்படை (Porunararruppadai) - 248 lines

4. பெரும்பணாற்றுப்படை (Perumpanarruppadai) - 500 lines
5. முல்லைப்பாட்டு (Mullaipattu) - 103 lines
6. நெடுனளப்பாட்டு (Nedunalvadai) - 188 lines
7. மதுரைக்காஞ்சி (Maduraikanchi) - 782 lines
8. நெடுநல்வாடை (Nedunalvadai) - 188 lines
9. குறிஞ்சிப்பாட்டு (Kurinjippattu) - 261 lines
10. பட்டினப்பாலை (Pattinappalai) - 301 lines

### 6 Main Themes:
- இயற்கை அழகு (Natural Beauty)

- வீரம் மற்றும் போர் (Valor and War)
- சரணாகதி (Devotion)
- ஆதரவு முறை (Patronage System)
- மனித உணர்வு (Human Emotion)
